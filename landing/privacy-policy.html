Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. What This Covers

This Privacy Policy describes how the Mini Numbers project website (this landing page) handles information. It also describes the privacy architecture of the Mini Numbers analytics product itself.

2. Landing Page Data Collection

This landing page:

  • Does not use cookies — no tracking cookies, no analytics cookies, no consent banners
  • Does not collect personal data — no forms, no email collection, no user accounts
  • Does not use analytics — we practice what we preach
  • Does not track you — no fingerprinting, no session recording, no heatmaps

3. Third-Party Services

This landing page loads the following external resource:

  • Remix Icons (via jsDelivr CDN) — for UI icons. jsDelivr's privacy policy applies to CDN requests.

No other third-party services, trackers, or analytics scripts are loaded.

4. The Analytics Product

Mini Numbers the analytics product is designed with privacy as its core architecture:

  • No cookies — uses sessionStorage for session management
  • No IP storage — IP addresses are processed in memory only and never written to disk
  • Configurable hash rotation — visitor identifiers rotate every 1 to 8,760 hours
  • Three privacy modes — Standard, Strict (country only), and Paranoid (no geo/UA data)
  • Self-hosted — all data stays on your server, under your control
  • Data retention — configurable automatic purge of old events

For a detailed technical explanation of the privacy architecture, see the Privacy Architecture Documentation.

5. Cookies

Neither this landing page nor the Mini Numbers analytics tracker uses cookies. The admin dashboard uses a session cookie for authentication purposes only.

6. Data Retention

This landing page does not store any data. For the analytics product, data retention is fully configurable by the server operator.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on this website, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you are a visitor to a website that uses Mini Numbers, contact that website's operator — they control their Mini Numbers instance and its data.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us or open an issue on GitHub.
